Before you sign in

Applicant readiness checklist

Gathering these items upfront reduces back-and-forth and helps Registrar and Accounting complete reviews faster.

  • Official school email and a strong password you have not reused elsewhere.
  • Legal name and birth details exactly as they appear on school records.
  • Program choice, category (new vs returning), and target term (year + semester).
  • Guardian or emergency contacts reachable during office verification windows.
  • For payment upload: clear receipt image (GCash screenshot or official acknowledgment).
  • Stable internet and a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari — current versions).

Security & privacy posture

  • Sessions are intended for authenticated access only; sign out on shared devices.
  • Uploads should contain payment or ID evidence you are authorized to submit.
  • The portal complements — not replaces — official notices from your program office.
  • Report suspicious account activity to IT immediately; never share passwords.
  • We design for least surprise: errors explain what to fix before you retry.

Answers at a glance

Frequently asked questions

Quick orientation for students and families. For case-specific rulings, always follow the latest memo from the Registrar or your college.

Who can create an account on this portal?
New applicants and returning students (typically second year and above) who have an official institutional email may register. If your email domain is not recognized, contact IT or the Registrar for provisioning guidance.
How long does each phase take?
Timelines depend on volume, completeness of your documents, and office calendars. The portal shows status so you can act on your side (e.g., upload payment proof) as soon as you are eligible — which often shortens total wait time.
Can I save my application and return later?
Yes. Use the enrollment form’s save workflow once required fields validate. You can sign in again from any device to continue, provided you keep your credentials secure.
What if my payment proof is rejected?
Read the notification or remark from Accounting, correct the issue (e.g., unclear image, wrong amount), and upload a replacement through the Payments view. When in doubt, visit Accounting with your official receipt.
Where do I get help if the site will not load?
Try another network, disable aggressive ad-blockers for this domain, and update your browser. Persistent outages should be reported to IT with a screenshot and approximate time — include your browser version for faster diagnosis.
Is there an assistant inside the portal?
Students see a guided assistant entry point after sign-in for common questions about phases and payments. It does not replace official decisions from staff; use it for orientation and next-step reminders.
Can parents or guardians access the portal on my behalf?
Credentials are issued to the student. Families may assist at the same device, but the account holder is responsible for accuracy of submissions and for protecting the password. Some offices may require the student present for in-person steps.
What happens after I am marked enrolled?
You should receive confirmation through official channels (email, bulletin, or class advisories). Keep copies of your approved enrollment summary and payment acknowledgments for your records.
